what makes us different?

At Defort cakes and more, we believe that baking is an art, and every pastry tells a story. What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to quality, creativity, and the personal touch that goes into every bite. Unlike traditional bakeries, we don’t just follow recipes — we craft experiences. Our ingredients are carefully sourced, always fresh, and where possible, locally grown, ensuring that every pastry not only tastes exceptional but also supports sustainable practices. We bake with passion, blending classic techniques with innovative twists to create unique pastries that are as beautiful as they are delicious.

What truly makes us different is our focus on customization. Whether you're planning a special event, a birthday, or just craving something extraordinary, our team works closely with you to design pastries that match your taste and vision. We take pride in making every occasion memorable with treats that feel personal and special. At Defort cakes and more, it’s not just about pastries; it’s about bringing people together with food that sparks joy. Come experience the difference — where every pastry is a masterpiece and every visit feels like a celebration.
